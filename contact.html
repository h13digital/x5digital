The X5 Audit Process: Transparency Before Transformation

We don't start with sales pitches. We start with understanding, analysis, and a clear path forward.

1

Deep-Dive Questionnaire & Discovery

You share your goals, challenges, and current marketing data. We analyze your tech stack, past performance, and market positioning to understand your unique context.

What You'll Provide:

  • Current marketing spend and ROI data
  • Business goals for the next 90-180 days
  • Access to analytics (Google Analytics, ad accounts)
  • Team structure and current bottlenecks
2

Live System Demo & Gap Analysis

We show you the X5 system in action and map it against your specific gaps and opportunities. No theory—just screens, workflows, and real examples.

What You'll See:

  • Live demo of the X5 5-Layer System
  • Gap analysis showing where you're leaking revenue
  • n8n automation workflows that could apply to you
  • Competitive analysis of your market position
3

Custom ROI Forecast & Tier Recommendation

You receive a 90-day action plan with projected operational savings and revenue growth. We recommend the precise X5 tier based on your ROI potential, not our sales goals.

What You'll Receive:

  • Custom ROI forecast with conservative & aggressive scenarios
  • 90-day action plan with clear milestones
  • Tier recommendation based on your LTV & operational maturity
  • Zero obligation to proceed—just clarity on your path forward
